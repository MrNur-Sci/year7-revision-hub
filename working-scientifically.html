1. Variables and Fair Testing

In an investigation, one thing is changed, one thing is measured, and everything else important is kept the same.

Independent variable

The variable you change.

Dependent variable

The variable you measure.

Control variables

The variables kept the same to make the test fair.

Scenario: ramp investigation

A student investigates how the height of a ramp affects the speed of a toy car. They use the same car, same ramp and same distance each time.

3. Lab Safety: Hazard and Risk

A hazard is something that could cause harm. A risk is the chance of harm happening and how serious it could be.

Safety scenario

A student heats water in a beaker using a Bunsen burner. They are not wearing goggles and their bag is on the floor near the bench.
