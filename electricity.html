1. Circuit Symbols

Circuit symbols make diagrams quick and clear to draw.

Cell

Provides a push to the charges in a circuit.

Lamp

Transfers electrical energy to light and heat.

Switch

Opens or closes a circuit.

Why do scientists use circuit symbols?

2. Series and Parallel Circuits

Series circuits have one loop. Parallel circuits have more than one branch.

Series

One loop. If one component breaks, the whole circuit stops.

Parallel

Multiple branches. Components can work independently.

3. Current, Voltage and Resistance

Current is flow of charge. Voltage is energy transferred per charge. Resistance makes current harder to flow.

Resistance model

Resistance 3 Ω

For the same voltage, increasing resistance reduces current.
