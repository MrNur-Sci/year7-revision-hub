1. Speed

Speed tells us how far something travels each second.

Speed = distance ÷ time

Distance

How far something moves, measured in metres (m).

Time

How long the movement takes, measured in seconds (s).

Speed

Distance travelled each second, measured in m/s.

3. Gravity, Mass and Weight

Mass and weight are not the same thing.

Mass

Measured in kg. It is the amount of matter and stays the same on Earth and the Moon.

Weight

Measured in N. It is the force of gravity on a mass.

Gravity

A pulling force towards the centre of a planet or moon.

Weight calculator

Mass stays the same. Weight changes because gravitational field strength changes. On Earth this model uses 10 N/kg; on the Moon it uses 1.6 N/kg.
